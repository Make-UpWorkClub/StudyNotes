[[Religion]] originated in [[South Asia]]
Inspired by [[Hinduism]] and [[Buddhism]]

Small but influential
Permits marriage

Principles:

- Ahimsa
	- Vegetarianism
- Self-Discipline
- Non-Possessiveness

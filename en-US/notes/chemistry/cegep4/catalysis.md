# catalysis

Use of a reactant (catalyst) in a [[reaction]] to decrease activation energy and increase [[vitesse de réaction|reaction rate]]

Types:

- Homogeneous catalysis: catalyst in same phase as reactants
- Heterogeneous catalysis: catalyst in different phase than reactants
	- Usually gaseous reactant is adsorbed on solid catalyst

> [!abstract] adsorption
> Collection of molecule on the surface of a material

# galvanic cell

Device that transforms chemical energy to electric energy

- Uses [[oxidation-reduction reaction|redox reaction]]
- Half reactions written as reductions

Consists of:

- Two half cells
- **Anode** (being oxidized) and **cathode** (being reduced) in each cell
- **Salt bridge** containing a strong electrolyte
- **Porous disk** allowing passage of ions

![[galvanic cell.png|300]]

> [!abstract] line notation
> Description of an electrochemical cell
> Anode (oxidation) and cathode (reduction) separated by double vertical lines
> 
> > [!example] $\ce{Mg_{(s)}|Mg^{2 +}_{(aq)}||Al^{3 +}_{(aq)}|Al_{(s)}}$

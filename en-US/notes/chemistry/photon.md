# Photon

[[Quantum]] of [[electromagnetic radiation]] (discrete energy packets of light)
`Syn.` light quantum

$$
\begin{align}
E & \propto v \\
E & = hv = \frac{hc}{\lambda}
\end{align}
$$

> [!abstract] Planck constant
> Smallest increment of a photon's energy
> 
> $$
> h = 6.33 \cdot 10^{-34}\pu{Js} = 4.14 \cdot 10^{-15}\pu{eVs}
> $$

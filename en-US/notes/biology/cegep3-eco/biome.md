# biome

Major ecosystem defined by [[climate]]

## Types

### Terrestrial

- Types:
	- Desert
		- Few precipitations
		- Least productive
	- Tundra
		- Permafrost
	- Tropical forest
		- Most productive

### Freshwater

- Types:
	- Lake
	- Stream and river
	- Wetland and marsh
	- Estuary

### Marine

- Types:
- Intertidal zone
- Oceanic pelagic zone
- Marine benthic zone
	- Coral reef: neritic
	- Deep-sea hydrothermal vent: abyssal

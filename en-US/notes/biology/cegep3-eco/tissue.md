# tissue

## Types

### Ectoderm

Skin and nervous system

### Mesoderm

Muscle and circulatory system

### Endoderm

Digestive tract

## By number

### Diploblasty

Has *two* germ layers (ectoderm and endoderm)

### Triploblasty

Has *three* germ layers (ectoderm, mesoderm and endoderm)

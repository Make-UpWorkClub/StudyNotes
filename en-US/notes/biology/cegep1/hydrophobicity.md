# Hydrophobicity

## Hydrophobic

*Repelled* by water
*Insoluble* in water
Tends to be [[Polarity|non-polar]]

Characterized by lots of *hydrocarbon* ($H$ &  $C$)

> [!example]+
> - $CO_2$
> - $O_2$

## Hydrophilic

*Attracted* to water
*Very soluble* in water

Characterized by lots of *oxygen*

Charged

## Amphipathic

Has both hydrophobic and hydrophilic regions

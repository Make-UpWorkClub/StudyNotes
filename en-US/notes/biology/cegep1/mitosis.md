# Mitosis

First part of [[mitotic (M) phase|mitotic phase]]
Division of [[somatic cell|somatic cells]] into somatic cells
Followed by [[cytokinesis]]

Properties:

- *1* cell division
- *2 identical diploid* daughter cells in humans

Phases:

1. Prophase
2. Metaphase
3. Anaphase
4. Telophase

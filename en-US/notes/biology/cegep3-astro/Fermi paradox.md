# Fermi paradox

Discrepancy between lack of evidence of [[life|extraterrestrial life]] and apparently high likelihood of its existence

> [!abstract] great filter
> Improbable milestone in the development of life preventing its advancement towards intelligence

## Hypotheses

- Rare Earth: planets with intelligent life extremely improbable
- Self-destruction: technological civilizations inevitably destroy themselves
- Zoo hypothesis: advanced civilizations exist but intentionally avoid contact with Earth
- Early birds: humanity is early because Earth forms earlier than most planets
- Different form: extraterrestrial life is so different that astronomers are looking for the wrong signals
- Interstellar travel impossible or impractical: EM wave still too slow for meaningful exchange
- Dart Forest hypothesis: extraterrestrial civilizations hide in fear of destruction by a hostile civilization
- Gaian bottleneck: most early lifeforms fail to quickly stabilize

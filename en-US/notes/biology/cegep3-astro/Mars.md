# Mars

[[Planet]] in the [[Solar System]]

## Atmosphere

- Thin
- No ozone => no protection from UV radiation

## Temperature

- Equator: 20°C during the day, -65°C at night

## Water

- Significant amount of water
- Mostly frozen
- Oceans in the past
- Evidence of seasonal flow of liquid water

## Locations

### Jezero crater

- Possible lakebed
- Landing site of Perseverance

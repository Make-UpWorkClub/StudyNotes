# thermophilia

Type of [[extremophilia]]
Love of extreme temperatures

- **Psychrophile**: <15°C
	- Snow algae: photoautotroph
	- Gryloblatta (ice crawler)
	- Elegant sunburst lichen
- **Mesophile**: 15-45°C 
- **Thermophile**: 45-80°C
- **Hyperthermophile**: >80°C

# escape speed

Minimum speed needed for an object to escape the gravitational field of a [[planet]]

$$
v_i = \sqrt{\frac{2GM}{R_i}}
$$

> [!note]- Proof
> $$
> \begin{align}
K_i + U_i & = K_f + U_f \\
\frac{1}{2}mv_i^2 & = \frac{GMm}{R_i} \\
v_i & = \sqrt{\frac{2GM}{R_i}}
\end{align}
> $$

## Examples

> Determine the escape speed of the moon.

$$
v_i = \sqrt{\frac{2GM_\text{moon}}{R_\text{moon}}} = \sqrt{\frac{1\cdot6.67\cdot10^{-11}\cdot7.348\cdot10^{22}}{1737000}} = 2.375\pu{\frac{m}{s}}
$$

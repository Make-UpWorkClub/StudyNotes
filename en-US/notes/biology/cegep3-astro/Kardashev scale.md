# Kardashev scale

Method of measuring the level of advancement of a [[life|civilization]]

- Type 1: able to harness all energy of the host *planet* and control all natural forces
- Type 2: able to harness all energy of the host *star* and colonize the star system
- Type 3: able to harness all energy of the host *galaxy* and colonize multiple star systems

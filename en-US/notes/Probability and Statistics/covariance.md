# Covariance

 Measure of joint [[variability]] of two variables

*Sample* covariance:

$$
Cov(X, Y) = \frac{1}{n - 1}\sum_{ i = 1 }^{ n } (x_i - \overline{x})(y_i - \overline{y})
$$

*Population* covariance:

$$
Cov(X, Y) = \frac{1}{N}\sum_{ i = 1 }^{ N } (x_i - \mu_X)(y_i - \mu_Y)
$$

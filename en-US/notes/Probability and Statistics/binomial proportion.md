# Binomial Proportion

Proportion of successes in a [[binomial distribution]]
`Syn.` sample proportion (of a sample statistic)

*Sample* proportion:

$$
\hat{p} = \frac{x}{n}
$$

*Population* proportion:

$$
p = \frac{K}{N}
$$

## Properties

$$
\begin{align}
E(\hat{P}) & = p \\
Var(\hat{P}) & = \frac{p(1 - p)}{n} \\
Z & = \frac{\hat{p} - p}{\sqrt{\frac{p(1 - p)}{n}}}
\end{align}
$$

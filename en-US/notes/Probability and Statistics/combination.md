# Combination

The [[counting#^5d51c4|cardinality]] by choosing $k$ elements from set of $n$ elements when order *doesn't matter*
`Syn.` binomial coefficient
`Pron.` "n choose k"

Combinations Rule:

$$
{n \choose k} = nCk = \frac{n!}{k!(n - k)!}
$$

# Electron Affinity

Energy *released* by *adding* an [[electron]] to an isolated gaseous atom
`Abbr.` EA

$$
\ce{X_{(g)} + e^- \to X^-_{(g)} \, \Delta H = -EA}
$$

*Horizontal* [[periodic trend|trend]]: *increasing*
*Vertical* trend: *decreasing*

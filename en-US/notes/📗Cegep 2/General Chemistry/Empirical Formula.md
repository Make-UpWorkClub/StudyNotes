# Empirical Formula

Simplest whole number ratio between elements in a molecule
`A.k.a.` simple formula

> [!example] Empirical formula of $C_4H_8O_2$ is $C_2H_4O$.

## Evaluation

To find the empirical formula of an unknown reagent knowing the products:

1. For each element shared only by the reagent and one product:
	1. Find percent composition
	2. Find mass
2. Optionally find mass of last element in the reagent by subtraction
3. Convert masses to moles
4. Divide moles by the smallest number of moles
5. Optionally multiply and round to obtain integer values

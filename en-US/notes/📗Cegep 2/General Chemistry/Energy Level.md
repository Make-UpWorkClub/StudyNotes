# Energy Level

Value the energy of an electron can take
`Sym.` $E_1, E_2, E_3, \dots$

## Types

### *Ground* state

Lowest energy level ($E_1$)
Most stable state

### *Excited* state

Higher energy level ($E_2, E_3, \dots$)
Excited atoms can absorb or emit energy in the form of [[Photon|photons]] to change state

## Energy sublevel

Character that determines the shape of [[orbital]] of an electron

# Hydrosolubility

Rules:

- Most are soluble:
	- Nitrate salts
	- Alkali metal and ammonium salts
	- Chloride, bromide and iodide salts
		- Except salts containing $\ce{Ag^+}$, $\ce{Pb^{2 +}}$, $\ce{Hg_2^{2 +}}$
- Most are insoluble:
	- Hydroxides
		- Except $\ce{Sr(OH)2}$, $\ce{Ba(OH)2}$, $\ce{Ca(OH)2}$ (marginally soluble)
	- Sulfide, carbonate, chromate and phosphate

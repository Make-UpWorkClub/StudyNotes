# Ionic Radius

Radius of an [[Modèles atomiques - Liaisons chimiques#Ionique|ion]]

*Cation* -> *less* than [[atomic radius]]
*Anion* -> *greater* than atomic radius

*Horizontal* [[Periodic Trend|trend]]: *decreasing*
*Vertical* trend: *increasing*

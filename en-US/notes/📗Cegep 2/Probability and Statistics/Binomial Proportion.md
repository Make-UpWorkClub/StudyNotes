# Binomial Proportion

Proportion of successes in a [[binomial distribution]]
`Syn.` sample proportion (of a sample statistic)

$$
\begin{align}
p & = \frac{K}{N} \\
\hat{P} & = \frac{X}{n} \\
\hat{p} & = \frac{x}{n} \\
E(\hat{P}) & = p \\
Var(\hat{P}) & = \frac{p(1 - p)}{n}
\end{align}
$$

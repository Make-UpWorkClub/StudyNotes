# Gandhi

A.k.a. Mahabma Gandhi
Born vaishya

Friend of [[Nehru]]

Coined the term "Harijan" ("children of god") in place of "untouchable", rejected by dalits

Assassinated in 1948

## Gandhian vision

- *Decentralized* government
- *Self-sufficient villages*
- No hierarchy
- Reform the caste system

- Revenge porn
	- `Aka.` image based sexual violence
	- Unconsensual share of sexually explicit material obtained through:
		- Private share by the victim
		- Hacking into the victim's device
		- AI production
	- Victim blame
		- Sex ed: if you take a nude photo of yourself, it's your fault
	- Laws:
		- Cyberbullying Bill
			- Problems:
				- Inconsistency: motive doesn't matter unlike other crimes
				- Only 20% of lawsuits lead to charges
				- Government is originally given access to user data without warrant
					- Privacy violation in the name of public safety
		- Proposed law to hold social media accountable for unconsensual porn and other hate speech
			- Problems:
				- Difference between consensual and unconsensual porn
				- Mass censorship
				- Death of privacy

	- Problems:

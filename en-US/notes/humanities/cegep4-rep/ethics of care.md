# ethics of care

[[visée éthique|Ethical framework]] that emphasizes the importance of care

- Formalized by feminists
- Does not replace but complements other moral rules

Four elements:

- Caring about (noticing the need to care) -> attentiveness
	- Ignorance and absence of attentiveness is a moral failing
	- Suspension of one's own plans, concerns and ambitions to focus on another's
- Taking care of -> responsibility
- Care-giving -> competence
- Care-receiving (response to the care) -> responsiveness

Disadvantages:

- Danger of dehumanizing the care receivers
- Impossible to be fully attentive

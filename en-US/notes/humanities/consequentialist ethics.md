# Consequentialist ethics

[[visée éthique|Ethical framework]] that focuses on consequence instead of intention
`Aka.` teleology

- **Summum bonum**: end goal
- Hedonism: physical pleasure is the only good
- Disadvantages:
	- No consensus on the summum bonum
	- The path doesn't matter
	- More complicated than expected

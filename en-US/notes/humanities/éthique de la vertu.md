# Éthique de la vertu (virtue ethics)

[[visée éthique|Ethical framework]] that focuses on rules and behavior that contribute to one's flourishing (reaching individual potential)

- Rooted in Aristotle's works and Confucious
- Aristotle: the highest good is the state of "living well"
- **Thin concept**: concept that evaluates without description
	- `E.g.` rightness, wrongness
- **Thick concept**: concept that evaluates and describes
	- `E.g.` truthfulness, cowardice
	- **Virtue**: positive thick concept
	- **Vice**: negative thick concept
- **Excellence**: state of having many virtues and few vices
- **Practical wisdom**: knowledge of how to use virtues
- Excellence and practical wisdom lead to collective happiness
- Advantages:
	- Takes emotion into account
	- Allows character growth by learning from virtuous people
	- Allows failure by learning from mistakes
	- Adapted to scenario
- Disadvantages:
	- Relativistic

Les vertus:

- Noblesse
- Courage
- Sagesse
- Discernement
- Honnêteté

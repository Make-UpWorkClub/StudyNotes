# p-Series

[[Series]] with the form

$$
\begin{align}
S_N & = \sum_{ n = 1 }^{ N } \frac{1}{n^p} \\
\lim_{ N \to \infty } S_N & \begin{cases}
\exists & \text{if }p > 1 \\
\text{DNE} & \text{if }p \le 1
\end{cases}
\end{align}
$$

`Syn.` Riemann zeta function

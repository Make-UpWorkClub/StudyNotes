# Chain Rule

Let $h(x) = (g \circ f)(x)$, then

$$
h'(x) = \frac{d}{dx}(g \circ f)(x) = g'(f(x)) \cdot f'(x)
$$

In other words, if we let $u = f(x)$,

$$
\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{dy}{du} \cdot \frac{du}{dx}
$$

# Series

[[summation|Sum]] of a [[sequence]]

> [!abstract]+ Partial sum
> The $N$-th partial sum of a series is the sum of its first $N$ terms
>
> $$
> S_N = \sum_{ n = 1 }^{ N } a_n
> $$
>
> The sequence of partial sums is a sequence in itself.

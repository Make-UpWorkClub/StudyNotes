# Group Riding

[[Cycling]] in group

## Tips

- Always communicate using hand or verbal signals

## Roles

### Leader

- Maintain a good pace
	- Ask verbally
- Lead in the right direction
- Watch out and signal obstacles

### Follower

- Pass signals

### Tail End

- Watch out and signal vehicles coming from behind
- Ensure no one is left out

# Bernoulli Distribution

[[probability distribution#Discrete|Discrete probability distribution]] of a [[Bernoulli trial]]
Special case of [[binomial distribution]] with $n = 1$

## Properties

$$
\begin{align}
P(X = k) & = p^k(1 - p)^{1 - k} \\
E(X) & = p \\
Var(X) & = p(1 - p)
\end{align}
$$

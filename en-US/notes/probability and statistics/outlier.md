# Outlier

[[Experimental data]] that is unusually different than others

Outside of:

- $[Q_1 - 1.5IQR, Q_3 + 1.5IQR]$ in statistics
- $[\overline{x} - s, \overline{x} + s]$ in lab

Causes:

- Mistake in observation or recording of data
- Belongs to different population
- Rare chance event

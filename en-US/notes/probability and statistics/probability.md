# Probability

(Study of the) likelihood of occurrence of an event
`Sym.` $P(E)$

## Conditionality

### Unconditional

Event has *no* given precondition

### Conditional

Event is given *some* precondition
Sample space is reduced to that of the precondition

$$
P(A|B) = \frac{P(A\cap B)}{P(B)}
$$

# Chi-Squared

Measure of difference between observed and expected frequencies used in [[chi-squared test]]
`Sym.` $\chi^2$

$$
\begin{align}
\chi_{(n - 1)(m - 1)}^2 & = \sum_{ i, j } \frac{(o_{ij} - e_{ij})^2}{e_{ij}} \\
e_{ij} & = \frac{e_ie_j}{n} \\
df & = (n - 1)(m - 1)
\end{align}
$$

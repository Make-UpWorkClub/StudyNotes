# The Island of Dr. Moreau

## Topics

### Vivisection

Dissection of living creatures

- Foreign practice introduced to England
- Perceived as cultural contamination by the public
- 1876: Cruelty to Animals Act
- Can observe the animal tissues in action

## Characters

### Moreau

Mad scientist

- Notorious for The Moreau Horrors
- Exiled from England
- Transforms animals into humans
	- Allusion to God

### Beast People

Human-like beasts

- Ugly and deformed
- Clumsy
- Capable of simple expressions
- Community:
	- Form primitive theocratic society
	- Has own culture
	- Has laws
		- Not necessarily followed
- Consider themselves as lower status humans
- Aspire to be like humans
	- Allusion to humans aspiring to be like God

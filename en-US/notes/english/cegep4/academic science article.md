# academic science article

- Purpose:
	- To rigorously describe the experiment
	- To explain and justify the importance of the experiment
	- To allow reproduction
- Structure:
	- Abstract
	- Introduction
	- Method
	- Results
	- Discussion
	- Conclusion
	- References
- Language:
	- Formal
	- Objective
	- Passive and detached
	- Jargon
- Peer review
- Issues:
	- Low reproducibility
		- 20-25% of cancer studies can be reproduced
	- Inappropriate practices
		- Hypothesizing After the Results are Known (HARK)
		- Selective reporting
		- Poor research design

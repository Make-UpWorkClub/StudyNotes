# image

Let $T:V \to W$ be a [[en-US/notes/mathematics/cegep4/linear transformation|linear transformation]]. The image of $T$ is the set of all resulting vectors.

$$
\mathrm{Im}(T) = \{ T(\vec{v})\ |\ \vec{v} \in V \}
$$

## Properties

> The image of $T_A$ is the [[column space]] of $A$.
> 
> $$
> \mathrm{Im}(T_A) = \mathrm{Col}(A)
> $$

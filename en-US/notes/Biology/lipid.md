# Lipid

Family of [[macromolecule|macromolecules]] consisting of fat, phospholipid, and steroid

Hydrophobic (insoluble in water)

> [!important] Not a polymer because it doesn't form a chain

> [!abstract] Fatty acid
> Long hydrocarbon (16-20 $C$ atoms)

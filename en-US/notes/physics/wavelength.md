# wavelength

Distance between two successive identical points on a [[wave]]
`Sym.` $\lambda$

$$
\lambda_m = \frac{\lambda_1}{m}
$$

> [!abstract] monochromatic
> (Of a wave source) only emitting one wavelength
> `E.g.` laser

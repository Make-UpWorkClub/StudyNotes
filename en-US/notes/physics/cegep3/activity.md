# activity

Number of [[nuclear decay|decays]] per second within the population

$$
R = rN = rN_0e^{-rt}
$$

> [!abstract] Becquerel
> SI unit of activity
> 
> $$
> \pu{Bq} = \text{decay/s}
> $$

> [!abstract] Curie
> Unit of activity
> Activity of 1 gram of radium
> 
> $$
\pu{Ci} = 3.7 \cdot 10^{10}\pu{Bq}
$$

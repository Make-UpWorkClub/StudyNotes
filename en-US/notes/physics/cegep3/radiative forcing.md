# radiative forcing

Way of quantifying the effect of various processes on the [[energy balance]] of the Earth

- $\Delta F > 0$ -> warming
- $\Delta F < 0$ -> cooling

Processes:

- Warming:
	- GHG increase
		- <= absorbs more UV and IR absorption
	- Ice melt
		- <= decreases albedo
	- Aerosol increase
		- <= reflects more sunlight
		- <= catalyzes cloud formation, increasing albedo
	- Positive feedback cycles
		- Higher temp -> more water vapour (GES) -> higher temp
		- Higher temp -> more ice melt -> higher temp
- Cooling:
	- Deforestation
		- <= increases albedo
	- Negative feedback cycle
		- Higher temp -> more water vapour -> more cloud -> higher albedo -> lower temp

> Small airborne particles

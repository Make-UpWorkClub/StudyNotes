# electric current density

[[electric current|Current]] per area of cross-section of conductor
Proportional to [[electrical conductivity|conductivity]] and [[electric field]]

$$
\begin{align}
J & = \frac{I}{A} = \sigma E \\
[J] & = \pu{\frac{A}{m^2}}
\end{align}
$$

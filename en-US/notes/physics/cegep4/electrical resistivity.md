# electric resistivity

Ability of a material to resist [[electric current]]
`Ant.` [[electrical conductivity|conductivity]]

$$
\begin{align}
\rho & = \frac{1}{\sigma} = \frac{m_e}{n_e e^2\tau} \\
[\rho] & = \pu{\Omega m}
\end{align}
$$

# capacitance

Charge stored in a [[en-US/notes/physics/cegep4/capacitor|capacitor]] per potential difference between the plates of the capacitor

$$
C = \frac{Q}{\Delta V}
$$

> [!abstract] farad
> Unit of capacitance
> 
> $$
> \pu{F} = \pu{\frac{C}{V}}
> $$

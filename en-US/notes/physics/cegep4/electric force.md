# electric force

Attraction between opposite [[electric charge|electric charges]]
Repulsion between same electric charges

- Conservative
- Directly proportional to charge
- Inversely proportional to distance

**Coulomb's Law**:

$$
-\vec{F_{21}} = \vec{F_{12}} = \frac{k_eq_1q_2}{r^2}\hat{r_{12}}
$$

- $k_e = 8.99 \cdot 10^9\pu{\frac{Nm^2}{C^2}}$: **electrostatic constant**
- $\hat{r_{12}}$: unit vector from 1 to 2

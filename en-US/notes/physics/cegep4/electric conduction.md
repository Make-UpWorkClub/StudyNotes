# electric conduction

[[électricité|Electricity]] by touching conductor with charged object

> [!abstract] discharging
> Removal of charge in a conductor by conduction to a large conductor (`e.g.` Earth)

> [!abstract] electric conductor
> Material that *allows* flow of electrons
> Valence electrons are *shared between all atoms* and are *free to move around*.
> => charge on a conductor *distributes* over the entire surface.

> [!abstract] electric insulator
> Material that *doesn't allow* flow of electrons
> Valence electrons are *tightly bound* and *cannot move around*.
> => charge on an insulator stays *immobile*.

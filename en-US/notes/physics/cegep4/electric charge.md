# electric charge

1. Intrinsic property of a particle that has a positive or negative [[électricité|electric]] influence
2. Property of an object that has different number of positively and negatively charged particles

`Sym.` $q$ (sometimes $Q$ for large charges)

> [!important] Conservation of charge
> Charge is always conserved in an isolated system.

> [!abstract] elementary charge
> [[Quantum]] of electric charge
> 
> $$
> e = 1.602 \cdot 10^{-19}\pu{C}
> $$

> [!abstract] coulomb
> Unit of electric charge
> `Sym.` $C$
